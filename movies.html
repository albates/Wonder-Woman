Wonder Woman On the Big Screen!

Wonder Woman (2017)

Gal Gadot's 2017 Wonder Woman grossed $821 million dollars world widemaking it the 10th highest grossing film in 2017. Actual production for the film began in 1996, at which point stars such as Sandra Bullock, Lucy Lawless, Mariah Carey and Katherine Zeta-Jones were consider for the lead role. After multiple delays and changes in script writers, Patty Jenkins was offered the opportunity to direct Wonder Woman, based on the story co-written by Allan Heinberg, Zach Snyder and Jason Fuch. With a film budget of $149 million dollars, Patty jenkins directs the DC Comics action thriller. Filming for the film began in 2015. Filming took place in England, France and southern Italy.

The film begins when Diana, the Princess of the Amazons rescues pilot, Steve Trevor, as his plain crashes in the ocean near her island home. Diana escorts Trevor back to London at a time when a war is raging in a world outside of her own. Diana believes that the war is influenced by the god, Ares. During her first experience with humanity, she seeks to promote peace and to fight evil, in an attempt to stop the war to end all wars.

Wonder Woman 1984 (2020)

The Premier of Wonder Woman 1984 brought new action packed sequences to Diana Prince, who is an archaeologist at the Smithsonian Institution, in 1984. Prince must identify stolen antiquities and comes across the "Dreamstone" that grants its owner one wish. While Diana unknowingly wished for deceased Steve Trevor to be alive, a fellow worker, Barbara Minerva wishes to be more like Diana Prince.

Ultimately, oil tycoon, Max Lord, comes in possession of the Dreamstone. The Dreamstone was created by Dolos, the God of Mischief. Lord wishes to be the Dreamstone in order to possess all of it powers in the hopes of becoming wealthy and powerful. Barbara and Max unite in their quest for power and to create havoc, while Diana must rescend her wish for Steve Trevor to be alive. Losing Trevor allows Wonder Woman to regain her strength in order to stop Barbara and Max's evil tirades. Wearing the armor of Amazon warrior, Asteria, Wonder Woman flies in to save the day!
