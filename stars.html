The Stars Who Portrayed Wonder Woman

Lynda Carter, Television's Heroine!

Lynda Carter was the first actress to portray Wonder Woman on television. Carter won the Miss World USA title in 1973 prior to winning the role of Wonder Woman in iconic television series. Wonder Woman was Carter's televison acting debut. As a child, Carter read about the superhero in DC comics. Wonder Woman's character provided inspiration for the young fan. Carter portrayed Wonder Woman between 1975 and 1979, on both the ABC and CBS televison Networks.

Carter has spent much of her life on screen and stage, as well as a recording artist. After Wonder Womanu, Carter appeared in multiple made for TV movies and televison specials. Recently, Lynda Carter has appeared in a variety of movies, stage perfomances such as Chicago, and performed voice overs for video games such as The Elder Scrolls.

Lynda Carter was married to Robert Altman for 37 years. Robert Altman passed away February 3, 2021. The two have two children together, Jessica and James. Carter continues to perform and sing, even as her 2020 Human and Divine Tour was cancelled due to Covid-19 regulations. Due to her worldwide recogniton as Wonder Woman, Carter continues advocating for the rights of women and girls, as well as others who have been positively impacted by Wonder Woman's character.

Gal Gadot, Wonder Woman of the Big Screen

Gal Gadot has become the big screen's newest Wonder Woman! Gal Gadot made her first appearance as Wonder Woman in the 2016 film Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice. Also, she appeared in 2017's Justice League. In a April 2021 article by Harper's Bazaar, Gal Gadot stated that she took some of her inspiration for her Wonder Woman Character from the late Princess Diana. Gadot wanted to emulate the late Princess' compassion and care for others through her portrayal of Diana Prince.

Israeli born Gal Gadot made her film debut in 2009's Fast and Furious as the character of Gisele. She would go on to star in Fast Five (2011) and Fast and Furious 6 (2013). She began filming for Batman v. Superman: Dawn of Justice in March 2014. Gadot made Wonder Woman's solo film debut at the world premier in Shanghai, China on May 15, 2017. The star continued Diana Prince's story with the film Wonder Woman 1984 in December 2020.

Prior to acting, Gadot served in the Israeli Defense Forces for two years. She was named Miss Israel in 2004. Other acting credits include the films Night (2010) and Knight and Day (2010), as well as several other roles for televison and film. Galdot is married and has two children.
