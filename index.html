All About Me!

I am a 17 year veteran teacher. I have taught multiple facets of the 9-12 social studies curriculum over my career. For most of my career, I have focused on teaching American History. Currently, I am teaching at a small charter school in western North Carolina. I hope to be able to design online instruction for universities and colleges, once I graduate from NC State University. When planning for classes, I like to use a variety of software and ed tech tools to enhance the quality of my lessons. I have found that these tools help to make the student's experiences in the classroom more engaing and enjoyable. Some of my favorite ed tech tools include:

  • Padlet
  • Flipgrid
  • Powtoon
  • WeVideo
  • Kami
  • Google Drawing
  • Newsela
  • Screencastify

I enjoy a variety of hobbies and activities when I am not teaching. I read legal thrillers by John Grisham, Greg Iles, Tess Gerritsen and others. I enjoying cooking and baking, having perfected an "Old Fashion" pound cake recipe. Lastly, I like to travel. My goal is to visit at least one historical site every summer!
